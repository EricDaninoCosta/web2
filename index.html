Commercial Diving Services

Subsea Brasil provides a wide range of commercial diving services for the offshore oil and gas industry, including:

  • Inspection, maintenance and repair of subsea structures and equipment
  • Underwater welding and cutting
  • Non-destructive testing (NDT)
  • Subsea construction and installation

Our experienced team of divers and engineers are dedicated to delivering high-quality services with a strong focus on safety and environmental responsibility.
